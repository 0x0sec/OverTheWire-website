Level 0

Level Goal:
Your goal is to connect to port 5842 on vortex.labs.overthewire.org and read in 4 unsigned integers. Add these integers together and send back the results to get a username and password for level 1.
Note: that vortex is on an x86 machine (meaning, a little endian architecture)
